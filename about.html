A little bit about me…

I enjoy adventures, learning, and trying new things.

Some of my favorite adventures include road trips around the country with my beautiful little family or going hiking in the woods with my dogs.

I’m currently attending Bellevue University to obtain a Bachelor of Science in web development. I thoroughly enjoy my studies! I previously graduated from Fontbonne University with a Bachelor of Arts majoring in advertising with a graphic design minor. I’m hoping to utilize all my skills going forward to help people/companies achieve their goals.

I never minded being terrible at something new. And so, I have a lot of different hobbies that I like to do. It all started with photography. When I was younger, my father gave me his old 35mm camera, a Pentax K1000. He ran a business with it and took pictures of his two kiddos; I took pictures of my friends skateboarding and nature. Naturally I picked up skateboarding too, which lead me into snowboarding. Then, I got this awesome internship with the St. Louis Blues as a photographer and I discovered the greatest sport on earth, ice hockey!

A quote that I like goes like, “fall down seven times, get up eight.” To me, this means to be relentless on whatever your aspirations are. Know that you’re going to stumble and be excited about each time you pick yourself back up.
